üìä Part 2: Essential Trend Indicators

1. Moving Averages (EMA/SMA)

TREND BEGINNER
üìç How to Add:

Insert ‚Üí Indicators ‚Üí Trend ‚Üí Moving Average

‚öôÔ∏è Recommended Settings for Beginners:
Parameter Setting Explanation
Period 20, 50, 200 Add THREE MAs with these periods
MA Method Exponential (EMA) Reacts faster to price changes
Apply to Close Based on closing prices
Color - 20 EMA Blue Fast MA
Color - 50 EMA Red Medium MA
Color - 200 EMA Black/Dark Gray Slow MA (overall trend)
Width 2 Makes lines visible
‚úÖ Best For:
  • Identifying trend direction
  • Entry signals on crossovers
  • Dynamic support/resistance
  • All timeframes (especially H4, D1)
üí° Pro Trading Tips:
  • Golden Cross: 20 EMA crosses above 50 EMA = BUY
  • Death Cross: 20 EMA crosses below 50 EMA = SELL
  • 200 EMA Rule: If price above 200 EMA = uptrend, below = downtrend
  • Bounce Trade: Buy when price touches 20/50 EMA in uptrend

‚öôÔ∏è Recommended Settings:
Parameter Setting

‚úÖ Best For:

‚ö†Ô∏è Warning: In strong trends, RSI can stay overbought (>70) or oversold (<30) for extended periods. Don't blindly sell when RSI > 70 in strong uptrend!

VOLATILITY INTERMEDIATE
üìç How to Add:

Insert ‚Üí Indicators ‚Üí Oscillators ‚Üí Average True Range

‚öôÔ∏è Recommended Settings:
Parameter Setting
Period 14
Color Orange or Yellow
‚úÖ Best For:
  • Setting stop loss distances
  • Measuring volatility
  • Position sizing
  • Not a directional signal!
üí° How to Use:

9. Fibonacci Retracement

TREND INTERMEDIATE
üìç How to Add:

Click "Insert" ‚Üí "Objects" ‚Üí "Fibonacci" ‚Üí "Fibonacci Retracement"

OR use toolbar button with Fibonacci symbol

‚öôÔ∏è How to Draw:
  1. Uptrend: Click on swing LOW, drag to swing HIGH
  2. Downtrend: Click on swing HIGH, drag to swing LOW
  3. Key levels appear: 23.6%, 38.2%, 50%, 61.8%, 78.6%
Important Levels to Watch:
  • 38.2%: Shallow pullback in strong trend
  • 50%: Psychological level, common reversal point
  • 61.8%: "Golden Ratio" - most important level
‚úÖ Best For:
  • Finding entry points in trending markets
  • Identifying potential reversal zones
  • Setting take profit levels
  • Works best on H4 and Daily
üí° Trading Strategy:
  • Wait for price to pull back to 50% or 61.8% level
  • Look for reversal candle pattern at Fib level
  • Enter in direction of original trend
  • Stop loss below next Fib level
  • Take profit at previous high/low or extension levels

üéØ Part 5: Beginner-Friendly Indicator Combinations

üî∞ Combo #1: Simple Trend Following (Best for Beginners)

Indicators:
  • 20 EMA (Blue)
  • 50 EMA (Red)
  • RSI (14)
Strategy:
  1. Wait for 20 EMA to cross above 50 EMA (Golden Cross)
  2. Confirm RSI is above 50 (bullish momentum)
  3. BUY when price pulls back to 20 EMA
  4. Stop loss below 50 EMA
  5. Take profit at 1:2 risk-reward ratio

üìä Combo #2: Support/Resistance + Confirmation

Indicators:
  • Draw Support/Resistance Lines (horizontal lines)
  • RSI (14)
  • MACD
  • Volume
Strategy:
  1. Identify key support level
  2. Wait for price to touch support
  3. RSI should be below 30 (oversold)
  4. Wait for MACD to cross up (bullish)
  5. Check for volume spike (confirmation)
  6. BUY with all confirmations
  7. Stop loss below support

‚ö° Combo #3: Momentum Breakout

Indicators:
  • Bollinger Bands (20, 2)
  • RSI (14)
  • Volume
Strategy:
  1. Identify Bollinger Band Squeeze (bands very tight)
  2. Wait for price to break above/below bands
  3. RSI should confirm direction (>50 for buy, <50 for sell)
  4. Volume should spike on breakout
  5. Enter in breakout direction
  6. Stop loss at middle band
  7. Ride the trend using Parabolic SAR for trailing stop

üöÄ Part 8: Next Steps

Your Learning Path:

  6. Month 4+: Once profitable on demo for 3 months, start with small live account.
